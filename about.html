My Story

Every journey starts with a question. Mine started when my accounts, passwords, and personal data were compromised. At first, I couldn't understand how it happened. The password was only known to me. So how did someone gain access? That incident sparked my curiosity and raised countless questions about cybersecurity, hacking, and digital security. Instead of accepting uncertainty, I decided to learn.

How It Started

I began researching cyber attacks, account compromises, social engineering, phishing, malware, data breaches, and security methodologies. Day by day, I learned how attackers think, how vulnerabilities are exploited, and how individuals can protect themselves online. The more I learned, the more I realized that many cyber attacks succeed because people simply aren't aware of the risks. That's when I understood that awareness is one of the most powerful defenses against cyber threats.

Why I Started SecureWithRatheesh

I created SecureWithRatheesh with a simple mission: To help people stay safe in the digital world. Today, I share cybersecurity awareness, ethical hacking concepts, online safety tips, and practical guidance that helps individuals recognize threats before they become victims. If even one person avoids becoming a victim because of something they learned from my content, then my mission is making a difference.
